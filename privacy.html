Privacy Policy for KaKa

Last updated: July 23, 2026

KaKa stores card information locally on the user's device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

KaKa does not use advertising SDKs, analytics services, tracking technologies, or developer-operated servers.

Information entered into KaKa, including card numbers, passwords, expiry dates, notes, categories, colors, QR codes, and barcodes, remains on the user's device.

KaKa does not process payments or provide financial services.

Device backups may be managed separately by Apple according to the user's iCloud and device settings.
